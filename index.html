Deployment of AI Applications for Cloud and Mobile Platforms

Prepared for: Vels Institute of Science, Technology & Advanced Studies (VISTAS)

Presented by: HERE AND NOW AI

"AI is Good"

About HERE AND NOW AI

HERE AND NOW AI is a leading technology organization dedicated to making Artificial Intelligence accessible, ethical, and practical. We specialize in educating the next generation of engineers and developers on how to leverage AI to solve real-world problems. Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex AI research and scalable, production-ready applications.

About This Program

This 10-day intensive training program is designed to equip students with the skills required to take AI models from a local development environment to a global scale. We focus on the entire lifecycleâ€”from containerization and cloud orchestration to mobile integration and performance optimization. Students will gain hands-on experience with industry-standard tools and frameworks.

10-Day Training Schedule

Time: 9:30 AM to 12:30 PM (3 Hours Daily)

Day Date & Day Topic Description
Day 1 Feb 2, 2026 (Mon) Introduction to AI Cloud Deployment Overview of AI lifecycles, introduction to major cloud providers (AWS/GCP/Azure), and setting up the development environment.
Day 2 Feb 3, 2026 (Tue) Containers & Docker Basics Understanding containerization, writing Dockerfiles for AI models, and local container orchestration.
Day 3 Feb 4, 2026 (Wed) Cloud DevOps for AI CI/CD pipelines for automated deployment, version control with Git, and introduction to registry management.
Day 4 Feb 5, 2026 (Thu) Building AI Backends (APIs) Creating RESTful and GraphQL APIs using Flask or FastAPI for serving machine learning models.
Day 5 Feb 6, 2026 (Fri) Cloud-to-Mobile Architecture Designing scalable architectures that connect cloud-hosted AI backends with thin-client mobile applications.
Day 6 Feb 9, 2026 (Mon) Managing Costs & Performance Strategies for reducing cloud compute costs, optimizing model latency, and managing auto-scaling.
Day 7 Feb 10, 2026 (Tue) Mobile AI Use Cases Deep dive into real-world applications: Computer Vision on mobile, NLP for chatbots, and personalized recommendations.
Day 8 Feb 11, 2026 (Wed) Edge AI & Modern Mobile Integration Exploring TFLite, CoreML, and ONNX for running lightweight models directly on mobile devices for offline capabilities.
Day 9 Feb 12, 2026 (Thu) Capstone Project: Development Hands-on project work. Students implement a full-stack AI application from backend to a mobile interface.
Day 10 Feb 13, 2026 (Fri) Capstone Showcase & Conclusion Project presentations, peer reviews, final feedback session, and certification roadmap.

Learning Outcomes

  • Deploy machine learning models securely on cloud platforms.
  • Write production-grade containers for AI workloads.
  • Design and implement API-driven mobile AI architectures.
  • Optimize AI applications for both cost and performance.
  • Deliver a full-stack Capstone Project demonstrating cloud-to-mobile AI integration.

Conclusion

Upon completion of this program, participants will be ready to tackle the challenges of modern AI deployment, ensuring their applications are not only smart but also scalable, efficient, and accessible across platforms.
